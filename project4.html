How to Make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Warm, gooey, and made from scratch in just five steps!

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

You'll need flour, butter, sugar (brown and white), eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, salt, and of courseâ€”chocolate chips.

My Original Photo

Step 2: Mix the Dough

Cream together the butter and sugars, then add the eggs and vanilla. Slowly mix in the dry ingredients and stir in chocolate chips last.

My Original Photo

Step 3: Scoop and Place on Baking Sheet

Use a spoon or cookie scoop to place evenly spaced dough balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

My Original Photo

Step 4: Bake!

Bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown. Keep an eye on them!

IMy Original Photo

Step 5: Cool and Enjoy

Let the cookies cool on the sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a rack. Enjoy with a glass of milk!
